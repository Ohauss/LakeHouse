Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Lake House reservations and experiences.

What's included in my Lake House stay? +

Your stay includes full access to the cabin with premium linens, fully equipped kitchen, all utilities, WiFi, outdoor gear, hiking trail maps, and 24/7 on-site support. We also provide organic welcome amenities, access to our garden harvest (seasonal), and complimentary morning coffee service.

How do I get to Lake House? +

The closest airport is Leknes (30 minutes drive). We provide detailed driving directions upon booking. Car rental is recommended for exploring Lofoten. For guests arriving without vehicles, we can arrange pickup service for an additional fee. The final 2km is a scenic forest road suitable for all vehicles.

What's your cancellation policy? +

Free cancellation up to 14 days before check-in. Cancellations 7-14 days prior receive 50% refund. Within 7 days, bookings are non-refundable unless due to extreme weather or emergencies. We offer flexible rescheduling options and travel insurance recommendations for peace of mind.

Is Lake House suitable for families with children? +

Absolutely! We welcome families and provide child-safe amenities, outdoor games, nature exploration kits, and family-friendly hiking suggestions. The property is secure with clear boundaries. We can arrange babysitting services and have partnerships with local family activity providers.

What activities are available nearby? +

Hiking, kayaking, fishing, Northern Lights viewing (winter), midnight sun experiences (summer), wildlife photography, foraging tours, traditional Norwegian cooking classes, yoga sessions, and guided meditation walks. We curate seasonal activity packages and work with local experts for unique experiences.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions? +

Yes! Please inform us of any dietary needs when booking. We can pre-stock organic, vegan, gluten-free, or other specialty items. Our garden provides fresh herbs and vegetables. We partner with local organic producers and can arrange for specialty deliveries during your stay.
