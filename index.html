About Lake House

LAKE HOUSE is an authentic spa hotel nestled deep in the forest. The dreamy landscape offers a peaceful escape from the noisy world. Guests who’ve visited once often dream of returning to its soothing, secluded atmosphere.

Our design is fully integrated into the natural surroundings and leaves no harm on the environment.

We’re proud to say not a single tree was cut during construction. Respect for nature is one of our core missions.

Our hospitality goes beyond the standard “bed and board” approach. The project is driven by clear goals:

  • Environmental protection
  • Preservation of forests
  • Organic vegetable cultivation

Our spaces are filled with natural light, warm wooden accents, and soft furnishings. Every window opens to breathtaking lake and forest views — designed to inspire, soothe, and reconnect you with nature.
